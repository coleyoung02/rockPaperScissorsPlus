Rules

Navigation Controls:

    Highlight Button on Left (Left hand open, Right hand pointing)
    Highlight Button on Right (Left hand pointing, Right hand open)
    Select Button (One hand open, One hand closed)

Game Options:

    Rock (One closed hand) - Beats Scissors and Water
    Paper (One open hand) - Beats Rock and Cannon
    Scissors (One pointing hand) - Beats Paper and Water
    Cannon (Two closed hands) - Beats Rock and Scissors
    Water (Two open hands) - Beats Paper and Cannon

How to Play:

    After starting the game, the player should choose one of the game options listed above.
    To lock in their choice, the player must hold this option for 5 seconds, shown at the top.
    After the 5 seconds are up, the computer will make a play as well. The game results will update.
    Try your best to choose the game option that will beat the computer!
